## 📦 Installation

1. Download **`FastConnect-Setup-X.X.X.exe`** from the [latest release](https://github.com/chaki53/fastconnect-pc-releases/releases/latest)
2. Run the installer and follow the prompts
3. Open FastConnect and sign in:
   - with a **6-digit code** from your phone (Account → "Sign in on PC"), or
   - with a **QR code**, or
   - with **Email and password**
4. Press the big button — you're connected ⚡

> 🆕 No account yet? Create one right in the app — **3 days free**, no card required.

## ❓ FAQ

<details>
<summary><b>Windows SmartScreen warns about the installer</b></summary>

Click "More info" → "Run anyway". The warning appears because the app does not yet have a paid code-signing certificate — this is normal for new software.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>How do I update?</b></summary>

You don't 🙂 — the app checks for updates on launch and offers to install the new version.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Connection dropped after my PC woke from sleep</b></summary>

Since version 2.0 the tunnel reconnects automatically after waking up. If you are on an older version — update.
</details>
